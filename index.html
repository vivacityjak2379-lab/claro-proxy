Four companions

Each one helps differently.

Choose based on what you need right now. Switch anytime in the conversation.

Claro
Mindfulness & mental clarity. Breathing exercises, body scans, journaling prompts. Calm and precise.
Friend
Genuine support, no judgment. Asks what you need first — to vent, or to think through ideas. Real and warm.
Therapist
CBT + ACT techniques. Cognitive restructuring, 5-4-3-2-1 grounding, behavioral activation. Validates before reframing.
Mentor
Diagnoses first, then acts. Eisenhower matrix, 5 Whys, Atomic Habits. Always ends with a clear next step.

Morning ritual

Daily check-in.
Three steps, one minute.

Each morning, check in with yourself before talking to your companion. It helps them understand where you are — and sends your state naturally into the conversation.

1
How did you sleep? — deeply, well, okay, or poorly. Your sleep shapes everything that follows.
2
Energy level right now — high, steady, low, or foggy. Not a mood, but a resource check.
3
One intention for today — a short sentence. Moves you from reactive to intentional.

Privacy

Designed to know nothing about you.

No accounts. No server-side storage. Your conversations stay between you and your device.

API key stays in your browser
Stored in localStorage on your device. Never sent to our servers. Never logged.
No conversation storage
Messages are sent directly to Anthropic's API and discarded. We never read or store them.
No account required
No email, no sign-up, no tracking. Open the app and start. That's it.
Quiz data on your device
Your onboarding answers are stored locally and used only to personalize your session.
